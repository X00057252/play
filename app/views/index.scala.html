Our School

Progress School of English was established in 2010 and offers quality general English language courses of all levels from Beginner to Advanced in a variety of programmes ranging from 2 weeks to academic year, as well as IELTS, Cambridge and TOEFL exam preparation courses.

The students are from various cultural backgrounds, mainly from within the EU, but also from countries like Brazil, Russia, Argentina, Korea, UAE and Egypt.

Our school is an ideal place to study English in Dublin. We are well situated on the South Side of Dublin, in Rathmines, one of the city's liveliest suburbs, just 10 minutes by bus from the city centre and served many different bus routes.

What We Offer Read more ⇓

We offer a range of English courses to suit students of all levels from beginner all the way up to advanced.

We believe in providing classes that help students in the 5 key areas of Reading, Writing, Speaking, Listening and Pronunciation.

Our weekly curriculum builds students confidence quickly and, with the help of our superb teaching staff, allows them to move through the levels with ease, all the time focusing on their individual learning outcomes. This means you make rapid progress with your language skills.

We will help you to set objective goals and measure your progress. We can really get to know you individually, giving you as much personal attention as possible.

School Facilities Read more ⇓

Our school offers a warm, welcoming and friendly atmosphere. The school building provides students with bright spacious classrooms equipped with video, large whiteboards and projectors.

There is also a Tea Room and Garden where students and teachers meet at break times to have coffee/tea with biscuits, to relax, read one of the magazines or just chat with other students from around the world. We have also a video room with a projector and surround sound speakers where students will be able to watch DVDs in the afternoon.
