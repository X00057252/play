Our Courses

We at Progress School of English offer a range of English courses to suit students of all levels. We have students from beginner all the way up to advanced.

The focus of our classes is communication. We believe in providing classes that help students in the 5 key areas of Reading, Writing, Speaking, Listening and Pronunciation. Our students are encouraged to use and improve their English at all times.

Our weekly curriculum builds students confidence quickly and, with the help of our superb teaching staff, allows them to move through the levels with ease, all the time focusing on their individual learning outcomes. This means you make rapid progress with your language skills.

We will help you to set objective goals and measure your progress. We can really get to know you individually, giving you as much personal attention as possible.

General English Courses Read more ⇓

This is our most popular course. It is also a basis for exam preparation courses.

General English course is designed to develop your Spoken, Written, Pronunciation and Listening Skills in English while also improving grammar and enriching your vocabulary. Lessons are based on everyday topics and are active, task-based and relevant to daily life.

You will be placed in a class that is appropriate to your level of English. You will take the level placement test at the beginning of the course and every 2 weeks afterwards.
