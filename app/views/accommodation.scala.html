Student Accommodation

Students can choose to stay with a host family or live on their own privately renting and sharing a house or an apartment. Staff at Progress School of English can offer advice on where best to search for accommodation and what areas are most safe and practical to live in.

Our Accommodation Officer is always there to help with any queries you may have.

Host Family Accommodation Read more ⇓

Students benefit hugely from staying with an Irish family. this is a perfect opportunity to converse and encounter new cultural experiences in a natural and warm environment. You will be welcomed into someone's home and cared for as one of the family. You will be exposed to language and culture in a variety of ways; for example - through conversation, television, radio, newspapers and magazines.

Staying with a Host Family

Host families provide breakfast and an evening meal from Monday to Friday and breakfast, lunch and evening meal at weekends. Irish breakfast nowadays is usually a light meal - fruit juice, cereal, toast and tea or coffee. Lunch on working days is generally a snack or sandwich. The main meal (usually meat or fish served with vegetables followed by dessert) is eaten at about 6 or 7 pm.

All host families are carefully selected and inspected by our Accommodation Officer before their registration with the school is fully completed and then they are regularly monitored once the student moves in with the family. They also live on or near a direct bus route to Rathmines.

We also gives a questionnaire to the families prior to registration so that they can indicate their availability and also their preferences regarding gender, nationality, age group, etc.

Booking a Host Family

Students who would like to book host family accommodation must fill in a questionnaire indicating their preferences or any special dietary requirements they may have such as being vegetarians, allergies, etc. during the course registration process.

Once the booking is finalized the student pays the accommodation fee to the school and we then pay the host family for the period of time booked through Progress School of English.
