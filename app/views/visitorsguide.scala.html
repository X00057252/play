Irish Hospitality

The Irish are renowned for their friendliness and for their tradition of hospitality. They have also earned a reputation for academic excellence over the years. Education and the skills of communication have always been highly valued. The Irish love to talk to visitors, to listen and to encourage others to speak English, an ideal environment for language learning.

Ireland has undergone rapid industrial and economical development over the past thirty years without losing its own distinctive identity or its strong sense of traditional values.

Below you will find a selection of information, links and suggestions on what to do in Ireland and Dublin.
